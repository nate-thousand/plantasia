How to Play

Use your computer keyboard to play the Plantasia synth.

25-key chromatic map (C3–C5) — same as MPK Mini:

Lower row C3–B3:

Z S X D C V G B H N J M

Upper row C4–C5:

Q 2 W 3 E R 5 T 6 Y 7 U I

Digits 2, 3, 5, 6, 7 are the black keys between the letters.
